How to Play

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game!

To play:

  1. Click on the "Play Game" button to start the game.
  2. Once the game starts, choose either rock, paper, or scissors by clicking on the corresponding button.
  3. After you make your choice, the computer will randomly choose its move.
  4. The winner will be determined based on the rules: rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock.
  5. The outcome will be displayed on the screen.
